⚡ Features

🖱️

One-Click Translation

Select any text on YouTube and instantly see the Chinese translation in the sidebar. No more copy-pasting into external tools.

🎤

Spoken Practice Mode

Hold the Z key to record your voice — practice spoken English responses to video content. Built-in microphone capture.

🎨

Smart Word Visualization

500+ English words are auto-matched with emoji illustrations. Learning vocabulary becomes visual, intuitive, and fun.

🤖

AI-Powered Translation

Supports OpenAI GPT and Google Gemini for nuanced, context-aware translations beyond basic dictionary lookups.

🌐

Multi-Engine Support

Switch between Youdao, Google Translate, MyMemory, and AI engines on the fly — choose what works best for you.

⌨️

Keyboard Shortcuts

Ctrl+Shift+Y toggles the sidebar. Hold Z for mic input. Designed for power users who live on the keyboard.
