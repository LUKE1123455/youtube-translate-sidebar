First, you install this internet plugin in your Edge or Chrome browser. Then, open a YouTube video and turn on the English subtitles. At this point, the sidebar will appear on the right side of the YouTube video page. Then, directly press Ctrl+C to copy the sentence or English word you want to translate, and it will automatically appear in the sidebar with Collins translation and example explanations. I also set shortcut keys: press D to delete and S to pronounce. The purpose of this tool is to help me learn English. My learning method is as follows: listen to the entire video without looking at the subtitles for the first time, then look at the subtitles for the second time. Listen once and if you don't understand, you need to look it up. At this time, this tool is very convenient. Finally, listen again without looking.
